# Privacy Policy Template For DocPDF

Last updated: [DATE]

## Overview

DocPDF is a document scanning and PDF creation app. The app allows users to scan documents, create PDFs, and share those PDFs from their device.

## Data Handling

- DocPDF processes document images on the user's device.
- Generated PDFs are stored temporarily in app storage.
- Temporary files are automatically deleted after the configured retention period.
- DocPDF does not require user account creation.
- DocPDF does not intentionally collect personal data on external servers.

## Camera And Photo Access

The app may use:

- Camera access to scan documents
- Photo library or gallery access when the user chooses to import existing images

These permissions are used only to provide the document scanning and PDF creation features.

## Sharing

When the user selects Share, the app sends the chosen PDF to the device's system share sheet so the user can choose apps such as email or messaging apps.

## Storage

- Scanned files are stored temporarily on the device
- Files are automatically cleaned up after the retention period
- Users may also manually delete files from inside the app

## Third-Party Services

DocPDF may use platform or SDK features required for scanning, sharing, and background cleanup. These services are used only to support the app's core functionality.

## Children's Privacy

DocPDF is not directed specifically to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
