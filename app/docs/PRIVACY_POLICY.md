# Privacy Policy for LovelyPDF

Last updated: April 4, 2026

LovelyPDF is provided by Grantham Enterprises.

This Privacy Policy explains how LovelyPDF handles information when you use the app.

## 1. Overview

LovelyPDF is a document scanning and PDF creation app. It allows users to scan documents, create PDF files, save them temporarily on the device, and share them using the device's system sharing options.

## 2. Information We Handle

LovelyPDF is designed to work primarily on your device.

Depending on how you use the app, the app may handle:

- Document images captured through scanning
- PDF files created from those images
- Temporary files stored in app storage
- Basic app preferences needed to support app features

LovelyPDF does not require account creation or user login to use the app.

## 3. Camera and File Access

LovelyPDF may use device features such as:

- Camera access to scan documents
- Access to selected images or files when you choose content from your device
- Temporary local storage to create and manage PDF files

These are used only to provide the core functionality of the app.

## 4. Local Storage

Scanned images, generated PDFs, and related temporary files may be stored on your device so the app can:

- Create PDF documents
- Show your saved scans inside the app
- Let you share or delete files
- Automatically remove expired temporary files

Files are intended to remain under your control on your device. Some files may be deleted automatically after the app's retention period or when you delete them manually.

## 5. Sharing

If you choose to share a PDF, LovelyPDF sends the selected file to your device's system share sheet so you can choose the app or service you want to use, such as email or messaging apps.

Any handling of the file after you share it is governed by the privacy practices of the app or service you choose.

## 6. Advertising, Data Collection, and Third-Party Services

LovelyPDF may display advertisements within the app. These ads may be provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob.

Advertising and analytics-related services may process limited technical information as needed to serve, measure, and improve ads and app performance. Depending on the service and device configuration, this may include information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, diagnostics, and ad interaction data.

The app may also rely on platform components, Google Play services, or third-party libraries that support features such as document scanning, file sharing, app stability, background cleanup, and ad delivery. These services operate under their own terms and privacy practices.

## 7. Notifications and Background Processing

LovelyPDF may use background processing to help maintain app functionality, such as cleaning up expired temporary files. If notifications are used by the app or supported by the device environment, they are intended only for app-related functionality and not for unsolicited marketing.

## 8. Children's Privacy

LovelyPDF is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly design the app to collect personal information from children.

## 9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep app data handling limited to what is necessary for the app's functionality. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 10. Your Choices

You can:

- Use the app without creating an account
- Delete documents from within the app
- Control what files you choose to scan, save, or share
- Uninstall the app at any time

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version should be made available through the app listing or other official source provided for the app.
